The outer-most part of the brain - [[cortex]] - is broken up into 4-5 major lobes (per hemisphere) based on major [[sulci]]. The most famous 4 are usually very familiar from survey courses on psychology or anatomy:
  - [[frontal-lobe]]
  - [[parietal-lobe]]
  - [[temporal-lobe]]
  - [[occipital-lobe]]
  - [[insula]]
